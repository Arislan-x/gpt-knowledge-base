Install from Chrome Web Store
SHA-256 verification is optional

Most users only need the ZIP. The `.sha256` file is not an installer. To verify download integrity, place both files in one folder and run:

Get-FileHash .\gpt-knowledge-base-1.3.7.zip -Algorithm SHA256

Compare the result with the value in the `.sha256` file. Continue only when they match; otherwise download the files again.

Product capabilities

From chat backup to knowledge assets

Preserve roles, sequence, and recognizable content structure so valuable conversations can become reliable inputs for retrieval and RAG workflows.

01

Cross-platform live capture

Automatically update the conversation currently open on a supported AI platform without proactively reading historical conversation lists.

02

High-fidelity local reading

Distinguish user, assistant, and thinking content while rendering Markdown, code, tables, citations, and LaTeX formulas.

03

Unified knowledge workstation

Group conversations by platform, combine browser backups with user-selected external sources, and keep the source clearly identified.

04

RAG-ready export

Export individual or selected conversations as JSON, Markdown, or HTML, including merged-file and per-conversation ZIP options.

Workflow

Build your collection in three steps

  1. 1

    Open a conversation

    The extension identifies the supported platform and continually saves the messages loaded in the open conversation.

  2. 2

    Open the workstation

    Browse backups from different platforms and inspect roles, ordering, formatting, and source metadata.

  3. 3

    Select and export

    Choose conversations worth preserving and export them as traceable source material for your knowledge workflow.

Privacy by design

Your data stays in your browser

The extension processes only conversation information already presented in the currently open page and does not proactively fetch account history.

Backups stay in local browser storage and are not uploaded to developer servers or cloud backup APIs.

You can pause live backup, export selected conversations, or clear local data at any time.

Only recognizable text and structure are backed up. Save images, video, audio, attachments, and generated files separately.
